# Replika

## Snapshot

- Category: AI companion app
- Company: Luka, Inc.
- Website: https://replika.com/
- Status: Active consumer product
- Last researched: 2026-06-21

## Product Thesis

Replika appears to believe the durable companion wedge is a persistent personal AI with memory, proactive follow-ups, voice/video access, avatar customization, and light life-coaching behaviors.

## Core Interaction Loop

The user chats with a persistent companion, customizes its avatar/relationship mode, returns for voice or video calls, and receives follow-ups or nudges based on remembered context.

## Job-To-Be-Done

Keep a persistent AI counterpart around for conversation, reflection, roleplay, and periodic prompts that feel personally timed.

## Memory Model

Recent App Store and Google Play copy emphasizes better memory, proactive check-ins, app integrations, and internet access. User-facing memory appears core to positioning, but exact memory architecture and deletion mechanics need deeper verification.

## Voice, Avatar, and Embodiment

Replika has one of the clearest avatar-led companion surfaces: mobile app, 3D avatar, customization, calls, and video-like interaction. This makes it the most relevant direct benchmark for whether visual embodiment changes perceived continuity.

## Retention Loop

Retention is built around persistent identity, accumulated memory, avatar attachment, proactive suggestions, and paid feature unlocks.

## Pricing and Business Model

Freemium with Replika Pro subscriptions and feature gating. Public app listings confirm in-app purchases; third-party pricing pages report monthly, annual, and lifetime plans, but current prices should be checked in-app before quoting.

## What Users Love

Positive signals cluster around always-available conversation, avatar personalization, calls, and the sense that the companion remembers the user.

## What Users Complain About

Recurring complaints in reviews and forums include pricing, changes to model behavior, memory inconsistency, and trust damage when paid or relationship features change.

## Strategic Lessons

Copy the seriousness about memory and embodiment. Avoid tying trust to unstable behavior changes or unclear feature gating. Replika is also a warning that companion users notice memory and personality regressions quickly.

## Open Questions

- How much of the new memory system is user-visible and editable?
- Which proactive suggestions are useful versus intrusive?
- How much retention comes from avatar customization versus memory?
