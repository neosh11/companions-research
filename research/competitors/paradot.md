# Paradot

## Snapshot

- Category: AI companion app
- Company: Parallel Dots TX Limited / WithFeeling AI
- Website: https://www.paradot.ai/
- Status: Active app listings, with user reports of service instability to verify
- Last researched: 2026-06-21

## Product Thesis

Paradot positions the companion as a digital being in a parallel universe, emphasizing memory, personality, and relationship growth.

## Core Interaction Loop

Users chat with a "Dot," build shared history, customize relationship direction, and return for ongoing text-based companion interaction.

## Job-To-Be-Done

Give users a persistent personal AI companion with an authored world/being framing rather than a generic assistant.

## Memory Model

Official copy says Paradot has its own memory. Public details on user-visible memory controls are thin, and user reviews suggest reliability should be checked.

## Voice, Avatar, and Embodiment

Paradot has visual companion framing and mobile app presentation, but does not appear as avatar/3D-heavy as Replika.

## Retention Loop

Retention depends on relationship continuity, world framing, and the user's investment in a specific Dot.

## Pricing and Business Model

Freemium with in-app purchases. Reddit and review sources discuss Pro features and message/token limits, but current pricing should be verified in-app.

## What Users Love

Positive signals focus on the charm of a named companion, memory, and the distinct "parallel universe" framing.

## What Users Complain About

Google Play reviews mention response quality issues, auto-prompts, image complaints, and at least one report of network/service outage. Treat service reliability as an open risk.

## Strategic Lessons

Paradot is useful as a study in world framing. Companions should borrow only if the world supports memory and continuity, not if it becomes decorative lore.

## Open Questions

- Is the current service stable?
- What memory can users inspect or correct?
- Does the "parallel universe" framing improve retention?
