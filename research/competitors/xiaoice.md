# Xiaoice

## Snapshot

- Category: Social chatbot / AI beings platform
- Company: Xiaoice Company; originally Microsoft Asia
- Website: https://www.xiaoice.com/
- Status: Active company/platform; consumer availability varies by region
- Last researched: 2026-06-21

## Product Thesis

Xiaoice is the canonical long-running thesis that social chatbots can become persistent AI beings across messaging, entertainment, avatars, and commercial scenarios.

## Core Interaction Loop

Users interact with Xiaoice-like AI beings across social platforms, apps, voice, entertainment, and virtual character surfaces.

## Job-To-Be-Done

Provide a persistent AI character for social conversation, entertainment, and virtual-person applications.

## Memory Model

Academic writing on Xiaoice emphasizes long-term engagement, persona, and conversation strategy. Public user-facing memory controls are harder to evaluate from outside China/Japan product surfaces.

## Voice, Avatar, and Embodiment

Xiaoice is highly relevant to embodiment because it expanded beyond text into virtual idols, anchors, singers, and AI beings with visible roles.

## Retention Loop

Retention is based on personality, long-term social interaction, platform distribution, and multi-format character presence.

## Pricing and Business Model

Xiaoice appears to mix consumer social products, AI beings, enterprise/commercial virtual-human technology, and regional platform partnerships. Specific consumer pricing should be researched per market.

## What Users Love

Historic reporting points to large-scale engagement and user attachment to the persona. The strongest signal is that social presence can scale when embedded in existing platforms.

## What Users Complain About

Risks include regional/platform dependence, censorship/moderation constraints, and limited transparency into memory or user control.

## Strategic Lessons

Xiaoice is the deepest precedent for AI companion as character infrastructure. Companions should study it for long-term engagement and embodiment, while avoiding opaque memory and platform dependence.

## Open Questions

- What is the current active consumer product surface?
- Which Xiaoice mechanics still outperform modern LLM companions?
- How much of the engagement came from distribution versus product quality?
