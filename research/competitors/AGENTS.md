# Competitor Research Instructions

Use this folder for product and company research on AI companion competitors.

## Scope

Competitor notes should focus on what the product teaches this lab:

- What job the product appears to serve.
- How the core interaction loop works.
- How memory, voice, avatar, and re-engagement are handled.
- What users appear to value.
- What users complain about.
- What strategic lessons apply to Companions.

Do not turn notes into generic company profiles. Keep every section tied to product strategy, research direction, or prototype decisions.

## Source Rules

- Prefer primary sources: official websites, app pages, docs, pricing pages, company blogs, interviews, and public announcements.
- Use reputable secondary sources for market history, funding, acquisitions, layoffs, usage claims, and controversies.
- Include source links in the `Sources` section.
- Clearly label uncertain claims as `Unverified`.
- Do not infer private metrics unless a source supports them.
- If information may have changed, verify it before updating a note.

## Note Template

```markdown
# Competitor Name

## Snapshot

- Category:
- Company:
- Website:
- Status:
- Last researched:

## Product Thesis

What does this product seem to believe about AI companionship?

## Core Interaction Loop

What does the user repeatedly do?

## Job-To-Be-Done

What job does the user appear to hire this product for?

## Memory Model

What does the product remember? What is visible or controllable by the user?

## Voice, Avatar, and Embodiment

How does the product use voice, visuals, avatars, or 3D presence?

## Retention Loop

Why would a user come back tomorrow, next week, or next month?

## Pricing and Business Model

How does the product make money? What features are gated?

## What Users Love

Summarize recurring positive signals. Include sources.

## What Users Complain About

Summarize recurring complaints. Include sources.

## Strategic Lessons

What should Companions copy, avoid, test, or ignore?

## Open Questions

What still needs verification?

## Sources

- Source title: URL
```

## Review Checklist

Before considering a competitor note done:

- The note has at least three sources.
- The `Last researched` date is filled in.
- Product claims are separated from interpretation.
- The strategic lessons are specific enough to inform a prototype or research decision.
- The note does not use unsupported market-size, retention, or revenue claims.
