# Candy AI

## Snapshot

- Category: AI companion / AI girlfriend platform
- Company: Candy AI
- Website: https://candy.ai/
- Status: Active consumer product
- Last researched: 2026-06-21

## Product Thesis

Candy AI's thesis is multimodal fantasy companionship: personalized virtual characters with chat, images, voice, and video in a private consumer product.

## Core Interaction Loop

Users select or create a companion, chat, request images/voice/video, and spend subscription or token credits on richer media.

## Job-To-Be-Done

Provide a polished, private, visual-first AI companion experience.

## Memory Model

Official pages emphasize personalized companions but give limited public detail on memory controls. Third-party reviews claim memory consistency, but that needs direct verification.

## Voice, Avatar, and Embodiment

Candy AI is highly relevant to visual companion UX: realistic characters, generated images, voice, and video are central to the offer.

## Retention Loop

Retention comes from character customization, media generation, premium tokens, and ongoing chats with a selected companion.

## Pricing and Business Model

Freemium/trial plus subscriptions and token purchases. Third-party sources report base subscriptions plus additional media/token costs, but current pricing should be verified at checkout.

## What Users Love

Positive signals focus on visual quality, realistic media, customization, and multimodal experience.

## What Users Complain About

Complaints focus on token costs, limited free tier, hidden effective cost of heavy media use, and privacy concerns common to intimate companion apps.

## Strategic Lessons

Candy AI shows how powerful visuals can be, but also how tokenized media can complicate trust. Companions should make 3D/avatar value recurring rather than purely consumable.

## Open Questions

- What persistent memory is available?
- How much do users pay after media tokens?
- Which visual features drive retention versus one-off novelty?
