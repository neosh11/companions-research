# Talkie

## Snapshot

- Category: AI character chat / creative AI community
- Company: MiniMax-linked product surface
- Website: https://www.talkie-ai.com/
- Status: Active consumer product
- Last researched: 2026-06-21

## Product Thesis

Talkie believes AI companionship is a creative character universe: users chat with, create, and explore many AI personas across text, voice, images, tags, and fandom-style categories.

## Core Interaction Loop

Users browse Talkies, chat with characters, create their own personas, use voice/text, and explore trending or themed roleplay spaces.

## Job-To-Be-Done

Provide an AI character playground for roleplay, creativity, fandom, and casual tasks.

## Memory Model

Public pages emphasize character creation and conversations more than durable personal memory. Memory controls and persistence need direct testing.

## Voice, Avatar, and Embodiment

Talkie has a stronger visual/AR/character angle than many chat-only products. App Store copy for Talkie AR emphasizes animated characters and AR-like presence.

## Retention Loop

Retention comes from character discovery, creation, events/tags, visual collectibles, and social-style browsing.

## Pricing and Business Model

Freemium with ads and subscription-style upgrades. Third-party reviews report Talkie+ around $9.99/month, but current pricing should be verified in-app.

## What Users Love

Users value character variety, visual presentation, and less friction to roleplay or create.

## What Users Complain About

Reddit reviews mention ads, paid edit features, and uneven value of subscription features.

## Strategic Lessons

Talkie is relevant to Companions' 3D conviction: visual character presence can be a product differentiator, but it needs memory/accountability depth to avoid becoming a novelty feed.

## Open Questions

- How persistent are character memories?
- How much does AR/visual presentation affect retention?
- What does MiniMax integration enable technically?
